FAQ

What is Austin Dynamix?

Austin Dynamix is a small, hands-on web team focused on helping people build websites that actually work for them. We keep things simple, personal, and direct: no salespeople, no tech overwhelm—just smart design, clean builds, and real support from the same people who build your site.

What do I need to provide before we start?

Usually just your content (text, images, and logo if you have one). If you’re not sure, we’ll walk you through it step by step.

How does the process work from start to finish?

We start with a conversation about your goals, content, and visual style. From there, we build, review, revise if needed, and launch when you're ready.

Can I make edits myself after launch?

Yes. Every site is delivered with editor access, so you can update content, images, and links without touching code. Or, if you prefer, you can stay on a Sync plan and we’ll handle it for you.

What if I need something that’s not listed in a package?

No problem. We can customize any package or quote a one-off solution depending on what you need—just reach out.

What happens if something breaks later?

If it’s something we built and it breaks, we’ll fix it—no charge. If the issue is outside our scope, we’ll still take a look and offer a clear solution at a fair, agreed-upon rate.

Can I use my own domain name?

Yes! If you already have a domain, we’ll connect it for you. If you don’t, we’ll take care of the process of registering one for you. Just note that domain providers (like GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.) charge a separate annual fee—outside of our control—but we’ll make sure to set it up correctly for you.

Is this better than using something like Wix?

If you're looking for a quick template, those platforms work. But if you want a site that's custom, clean, fast, and fully yours—with real support behind it—you’ll get a lot more value here. No hidden platform fees, no design limitations, and no dealing with generic templates. Plus, we'll be here with you every step of the way!

What if I want something that’s not in the package?

No problem. We can quote custom features, extra pages, or advanced design as needed. Just ask, we're flexible!

Why should I choose Austin Dynamix over a freelancer?

You get direct access to the people actually building your site. We're small, fast, personal, and focused on quality. At an affordable cost. That means your project gets more attention and better results.

What happens after my site is live?

You’ll receive full access and ownership, plus the tools to edit it yourself. If you want help down the road, we’re still here. You won’t be abandoned!

How do I get started?

Simple—just reach out through the contact form. We’ll schedule a quick conversation to understand what you need, walk you through the options, and figure out the best fit. There’s no pressure, no jargon—just a real talk about what makes sense for your site. Once you're ready, we’ll guide you step by step.
