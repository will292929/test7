What is the Elite Plan?

The Elite package is a fully managed website experience, tailored to your needs, goals, and pace. It’s not just a build—it’s an ongoing partnership.

We start with a one-on-one conversation to map out what you need, whether it’s unlimited pages, custom features, regular updates, or strategy support. You’ll get hosting, updates, content support, and design evolution—all handled by the same team that built your site. No handoffs. No headaches.

Perfect for brands, teams, and professionals who want their website to grow alongside their business—without managing it themselves.

What You Get:
Custom-scoped, unlimited-page website built to fit your goals
Fully managed updates, design enhancements, and feature rollouts
Domain and hosting setup, monitoring, and optimization
Hands-on collaboration with real humans—no outsourcing, no bots
Flexible pricing—billed monthly or one-time depending on your needs

The Key Features

The Elite package is designed for clients whose needs go beyond the Launch or Command tiers. Maybe you need more than five pages. Maybe your content doesn’t fit a linear format. Maybe you want layered navigation, dynamic layouts, or a design that breaks convention. Whatever the case, Elite is where we stop guessing and start building around you.

There are no page limits, no fixed layout assumptions, and no pre-boxed feature lists. We’ll define the structure together—whether that’s 10+ pages, multiple sub-navigation levels, product or portfolio showcases, or something entirely custom. This package is ideal for large or content-heavy sites, complex site maps, or anyone who simply needs more space to work with.

With Elite, we can build in advanced visual elements, content blocks, and page functionality that don’t fit within a typical static structure. That could mean dynamic sections, anchor navigation, detailed service breakdowns, interactive visuals, or high-contrast content areas that change how users experience your brand. The design is yours—it’s shaped around your material, your goals, and how you want people to engage with your site.

We price Elite based on scope—there’s no set cost because there’s no standard version of this package. After a one-on-one planning session, we’ll build out a roadmap and determine whether a one-time project, phased rollout, or ongoing build model makes the most sense. Whether you’re a scaling business, a growing brand, or a creator with big ideas, Elite gives you a space to do it right—with us in your corner the whole way.
