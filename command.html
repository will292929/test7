What is the Command Plan?

The Command package gives you a fully custom multi-page website, built from the ground up to showcase your content, services, or brand with structure and style.

You get up to five cohesive pages, professionally designed for clarity, speed, and consistency—everything connected, everything intentional. We handle the domain, hosting setup, and deliver the finished site with an editor so you can manage it without needing to code.

Perfect for small businesses, growing creators, service providers, or anyone who needs more than a single page to make an impact.

What You Get:
Up to five custom-designed static pages
Responsive design with seamless navigation and visual consistency
Domain and GitHub-based hosting setup included
Delivered with an editor for easy content updates
Clean, flexible, and scalable—without platform bloat or subscription fees

The Key Features

Command gives you a full multi-page website—designed from scratch to support your message, services, and future growth. It includes up to five fully custom pages, each built with a clear structure and visual flow. Whether you're showcasing work, listing services, or building trust, Command gives you the space and flexibility to say more without losing focus.

Every page in your Command build is visually consistent, professionally styled, and aligned with your brand identity. From typography to layout to color balance, we tie it all together so your site feels complete and intentional—not stitched together.

We handle the domain registration, hosting connection, and project deployment—just like in Launch. Your site runs on GitHub-based hosting for speed and simplicity, and you’ll own the domain and files entirely. You won’t be locked into any platform or service.

Once the site is live, you’ll have access to a simple visual editor across all your pages. You can update text, swap images, and adjust links easily—or stay on a Sync plan and let us handle the upkeep. Command gives you room to grow without technical barriers.
